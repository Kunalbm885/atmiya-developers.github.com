ABOUT US

Who We Are ...

Atmiya Developers was conceptualized to help people meet their dreams with reality. Since its inception on August 17th 1991, Atmiya Developers has endeavoured to turn houses into homes and offices into productive workspaces which blend elegance, functionality, comfort, conveniences, proximity and affordability.

With over three decades of experience in the Real Estate Sector, we have been using our skills and expertise to dig deep and stand high. Before transforming their wishes into reality, we care to understand what modern-day customers really want. Staying in the loop with the changing times, we aim to set higher benchmarks for customer relationships defined by sturdy and sophisticated structures.

We have provided beautiful and luxurious homes to happy customers and are continuously working hard to serve many more. We welcome you to find your future home with us. We believe that everyone deserves a chance at high-standard living and to experience the finer things in life. We hope you choose Atmiya Developers to build you the nest of your dreams.

WHY Choose Us ..

CUTTING-EDGE AESTHETICS AND STYLE

We believe in doing things in style, and that is why our homes are not just places to live but masterpieces to gaze at with awe. We love to play around with myriad architectural styles and colors and bring to you a magnificent home that can rival all the architectural wonders of the world.

SMART DESIGN PLAN

Our homes are planned to perfection to ensure fresh air and natural light at all times. We have meticulously planned each living unit to allow for smart and optimal usage of space. Even the common areas have been planned in a way that facilitates social interaction while also allowing for privacy, but not complete isolation.

STANDARDS OF SAFETY

Our homes meet all the local and international standards of safety. We have an effective disaster management system in place as well as strategically placed emergency exits in case of the unforeseen. Atmiya Developers projects are routinely inspected for compliance with safety norms and statutes, and have been internationally certified for being 100% safe and damage-proof.

HAPPY, HOLISTIC LIVING

Atmiya Developers homes are located in close proximity with nature, far removed from the hustle and bustle of the city, while also being fully equipped with all the necessities of modern city life. We have pristine green spaces all around as well as premium facilities like swimming pools, clubhouses, recreation clubs, and designated common spaces for social activities.

Founders

Founder Desk

Naresh Joshi

Founder

Mr. Naresh Joshi wanted to combine his entrepreneurial acumen with his technical prowess and thus led to the start of his firm in 1991. Mr. Joshi is known for his infectious calm demeanour, breaking stereotypes on operational and technical levels and always staying one step ahead. His hunger for perfection and achieving excellence in anything he undertakes makes him stand out in the industry. Beyond his firm’s doors, he takes an intense interest in reading philosophy and invests his time in fitness and good health.

Hemang Joshi

Managing Director

Having completed his Bachelor of Science in Chemistry and supplementing this with eighteen years of work experience in the Kutch - Gujrat, Mr. Hemang Joshi brings a fresh perspective to the firm. After sharpening his skills by working with illustrious clients like Frito Lay, Mr. Joshi wants to contribute to the home firm and aspires to multiply the success ratio. He is a firm believer in the dictum that there is no replacement for hard work and that the right work ethic can take you places. Besides his go-getter attitude, he takes pride in being a cricket enthusiast, a nature lover and an avid traveller.
